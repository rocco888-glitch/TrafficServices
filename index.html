What We Handle

We fight ticketsso you don't have to.

From minor speeding charges to serious driving offences — our first priority is always to get your case dismissed entirely. If dismissal isn't possible, we fight hard to challenge the evidence and get your charges reduced.

01

Speeding Tickets

Our first goal is always a full dismissal. If that's not achievable, we challenge the evidence and negotiate a reduction to protect your record and keep your insurance low.

02

Careless Driving

Careless driving charges carry heavy fines and demerit points. We first pursue a full dismissal — if that's not possible, we challenge the evidence and fight to get the charge reduced or withdrawn.

03

Stunt Driving

One of Ontario's most serious traffic offences. We push first for a full dismissal, and where that isn't possible, we dig into the evidence to minimize the consequences and protect your licence.

04

Red Light & Stop Sign

Don't let a simple ticket follow you. We pursue dismissal first — and if that's not on the table, we work to reduce the charge before it touches your record.

05

Licence Suspensions

Lost your licence or at risk of suspension? We first seek to have the suspension dismissed or overturned entirely. If that's not possible, we fight to reinstate your driving privileges as quickly as possible.

06

All Traffic Violations

HTA offences, distracted driving, following too closely, improper lane change — whatever the charge, our approach is the same: dismiss it first, reduce it if we have to.

About

Traffic Ticket FearStops Here.

Ian

Owner — Traffic Services Bradford

A flashing light in your rearview mirror can strike fear into even the most careful driver — but being pulled over doesn't have to mean paying that fine. At Traffic Services Bradford, we fight to get your charges dismissed or reduced so you can keep your record clean, your insurance low, and your licence in hand.

Ian Boteler has been serving Bradford and the surrounding area since 1998, partnering with original founder John Harrison before taking over the business entirely in 2008. What sets Traffic Services apart is a personal, hands-on approach — Ian works directly with every client, keeps them informed throughout the process, and makes them feel like a partner, not just a file number.

"It's very gratifying to be able to make a difference in people's lives," Ian says. "We try to take the pressure off — to make our clients feel at ease."

In most cases, clients don't even need to appear in court. Traffic Services handles everything — the filings, the negotiations, the court appearances — so you can stay employed, keep driving, and get on with your life.
